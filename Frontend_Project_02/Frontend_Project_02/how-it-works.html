Three habits, one shelf

Marginalia isn't a subscription you forget about. It's a small loop: choose, read, discuss — sized to fit around your day, not the other way around.

01 — choose

Pick a title

Browse the shelf by mood, not just genre. Every book comes with a short, honest note on who it's for and how long it takes to finish.

02 — read

Read in short sittings

The reader is built for the ten minutes you actually have — on a train, in a queue, before sleep — not the free weekend you don't.

03 — discuss

Talk it over, or don't

Join a small reading circle when you finish a book, or skip straight to the next one. Both are completely normal here.

Common questions

Before you start

How much does it cost?

Books are priced individually, usually between $9 and $16. There's no subscription fee — the monthly newsletter recommendation is free.

Can I read offline?

Yes. Once a book is added to your shelf it's available offline on every device you're signed into.

What if I don't finish a book?

Nothing happens. There are no streaks, reminders, or guilt emails. Pick it back up whenever, or don't.

How do reading circles work?

Circles are small groups of 6–10 readers who finished the same book in the same month. Joining is optional and always free.
